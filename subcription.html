Subscription Terms

Last updated: January 2025

1. Subscription Overview

SwiftReceipt offers a premium subscription that unlocks advanced features for professional receipt management.

2. Subscription Plans

2.1 Free Tier

Included Features:

  • Up to 25 products in inventory
  • Up to 300 receipts per month
  • Basic receipt templates
  • Standard thermal printing
  • Basic analytics
  • Email support

2.2 Professional Subscription

Premium Features:

  • Unlimited products and receipts
  • Advanced analytics and reporting
  • Custom receipt template editor
  • Barcode template editor
  • Advanced thermal printing options
  • Priority support
  • Early access to new features

3. Pricing and Billing

3.1 Pricing

Subscription pricing varies by region and currency:

  • United States: $2.99/month
  • European Union: €2.99/month
  • United Kingdom: £2.49/month
  • Canada: CA$3.99/month
  • Australia: A$4.49/month
  • Japan: ¥350/month
  • India: ₹199/month
  • Other regions: Local equivalent pricing

3.2 Billing Cycle

  • Subscriptions are billed monthly
  • Billing occurs on the same date each month
  • Charges appear on your App Store account

3.3 Payment Processing

  • All payments are processed by Apple's App Store
  • We do not store or process payment information
  • Payment methods are managed through your Apple ID

4. Auto-Renewal

4.1 Automatic Renewal

Your subscription will automatically renew unless:

  • You cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date
  • Your payment method fails
  • We discontinue the subscription service

4.2 Renewal Notifications

Apple will send you renewal notifications before each billing cycle.

5. Cancellation and Refunds

5.1 How to Cancel

  • Go to your iPhone/iPad Settings
  • Tap your Apple ID
  • Tap "Subscriptions"
  • Find SwiftReceipt and tap "Cancel Subscription"

5.2 Cancellation Effects

  • You will continue to have access until the end of your current billing period
  • No refunds are provided for partial months
  • Your data remains accessible but premium features will be disabled

5.3 Refund Policy

  • Refunds are handled by Apple's App Store
  • Contact Apple Support for refund requests
  • We cannot process refunds directly

6. Subscription Management

6.1 Account Access

Premium features are available on all devices where you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

6.2 Feature Availability

  • Premium features are unlocked immediately after purchase
  • Features remain available until subscription expires
  • Some features may require app restart to activate

7. Changes to Subscription

7.1 Price Changes

If we change subscription prices:

  • You will be notified at least 30 days in advance
  • Changes will apply to your next billing cycle
  • You can cancel before the price change takes effect

7.2 Feature Changes

We may add, modify, or remove features with notice to subscribers.

8. Special Offers

8.1 Early Tester Access

Early TestFlight testers may receive:

  • Free 3-month access to premium features
  • Special promotional pricing
  • Early access to new features

8.2 Promotional Offers

We may offer promotional pricing or free trials from time to time.

9. Support and Service

9.1 Premium Support

Professional subscribers receive:

  • Priority email support
  • Faster response times (24-48 hours)
  • Direct access to development team

9.2 Service Availability

We strive for 99.9% uptime but cannot guarantee uninterrupted service.
