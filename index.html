❓ Common Questions

  • How do I connect my thermal printer? Use the printer scanner in the app settings.
  • Can I export my data? Yes, you can export receipts as PDF or CSV.
  • Is my data secure? All data is encrypted and stored locally on your device.
  • Do you support multiple currencies? Yes, SwiftReceipt supports global currencies.
  • How do I cancel my subscription? Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions and cancel SwiftReceipt.
  • Can I get a refund? Contact Apple Support through reportaproblem.apple.com
